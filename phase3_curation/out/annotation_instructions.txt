HOW TO RATE THESE POSTS
=======================

WHAT WE'RE DOING
We are checking the quality of a collection of social-media posts. We want to know,
for each post: does it actually say something, or is it empty? Please read each post
and give it a rating of 0, 1, or 2 using the guide below. There are 200 posts.

You are rating ONE thing only: how much real content the post has. You are NOT
rating whether you agree with it, whether it's well written, whether it's about an
interesting topic, or whether you personally like it. A post you disagree with can
still be a 2. A post about a boring subject can still be a 2.


THE THREE RATINGS

0  =  EMPTY
      The post doesn't really say anything. It's just a mood, a reaction, a greeting,
      a caption, or hype. If you removed it, no one would have missed any information
      or idea.
      Examples:
        - "feeling so tired today, send coffee"
        - "this show is SO good omg I can't"
        - "new haircut, what do we think??"
        - "Day 4 of posting until they notice me"

1  =  SOMETHING THERE
      The post makes a real point or shares a real bit of experience, but it's
      ordinary or light. A specific observation, a personal story, a plain opinion, a
      simple tip. You learn or get something small from it, even if it's not deep.
      Examples:
        - "Took the long way home past the old mill. They finally repainted it blue."
        - "Aldi's frozen croissants are genuinely better than the bakery ones near me."
        - "I think the new season is weaker because they split the main cast up too early."

2  =  STRONG
      The post gives you something worth reading: a real insight, a clear argument
      with a reason behind it, a useful piece of knowledge, or a vivid, well-made
      observation. After reading it you've actually learned or thought about something.
      Examples:
        - "The reason these batteries degrade fast isn't the cold, it's the charging
           cycles you do while they're still cold. Warm them first and they last years."
        - "Calling it a 'labor shortage' hides the real story: wages stopped tracking
           productivity in the 1970s, so the jobs exist, just not at livable pay."
        - "People miss that the 1947 rule change is why the position exists at all -
           before it, this kind of play was simply illegal."


HOW TO DECIDE WHEN YOU'RE UNSURE
- Between 0 and 1: if the post has ANY real, specific content (a fact, a story, a
  concrete opinion), give it a 1. Save 0 for posts that are purely mood, hype, or a
  bare caption.
- Between 1 and 2: give a 2 only if you genuinely got an idea, an argument, or a
  useful piece of knowledge. If it's just a nice ordinary observation, it's a 1.
- Don't overthink it. Go with your first honest read. Most posts will be 1s; that's
  expected. Spend no more than a few seconds per post.


HOW TO FILL IT IN
Open annotation_posts.csv. For each row, read the "text" column and type 0, 1, or 2
in the "rating" column. Do not change the "id" or "text" columns. Save the file when
you're done and send it back. Please rate every row.

Thank you!
